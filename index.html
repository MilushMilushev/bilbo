About Us

Established in 1996 as a family-run honey production business, Zet 1 Ltd has maintained its identity and company values thanks to our guiding principle of high quality production. That also helps us keep our customers happy.

Our mission is to deliver raw honey of the highest quality to the end consumer. The key to fulfilling our mission is to select our honey from local bee farmers.

We’ve been offering bio-certified produce since 2014. Every different type of honey has a distinct taste, colour, scent and story. We select 6 types of honey produced from bees located in East Bulgaria. Find them below.
